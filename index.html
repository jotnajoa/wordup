Number of Unique*words

How Unique* words set was made

Word Count:

Over the last 40 years, raps have used maximum 2,400 unique words in one song and minimum 3 unique words in one song.

Take the outliers, top and bottom 20% of the distribution, to get the overall tendency of unique words counts within songs.

Out of entire lyrics from a song, remove any duplicates of words used more than twice

After leaving the word duplicates, what's left are counted as Unique-word*
